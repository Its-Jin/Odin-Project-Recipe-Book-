Birria Tacos

Birria Tacos are a show-stopping dish that is juicy, cheesy, and salty. It is a Mexican dish that consists of a thick stew stuffed in a corn tortilla and pan-fried. This is my take on the dish and this recipe is not authentic. I only cook for my family and friends using ingredients that are readily available to me. If you do try the recipe, let me know your thoughts! Happy eating!

Ingredients

Sauce

  1. 7 Ancho chile
  2. 7 Guajillo chile
  3. 3 Chipotle chile
  4. 1 White onion
  5. 7 Garlic cloves
  6. 1 Orange
  7. 2 Green Onion
  8. 3 sprigs Cilantro
  9. 1 tsp Cumin
  10. 1 tsp Paprika
  11. 1 tsp Mexican Oregano
  12. 1 tsp Instant coffee
  13. 1 tsp Cocoa powder
  14. 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  15. 3 tbsp Fish Sauce
  16. 1 tsp Honey/Agave
  17. 1 tsp neutral oil
  18. 2 cup Broth/Water
  19. Salt and pepper to taste

Stew

  1. 3 lbs Short Ribs

Taco

  1. 1/4 cup chopped Cilantro
  2. 1/4 White onion chopped
  3. 1 lime
  4. 12 corn tortillas
  5. 3 oz Oaxacan cheese or mozzarella

Instructions

Sauce

  1. Deseed and destem all chilies. Toast chiles in a hot oiled pan until colour darkens. Set aside to cool.
  2. Chop onion, garlic, green onion, cilantro into medium size chunks and add to a food processor.
  3. Juice the orange and add it to the food processor.
  4. Add all spices, oregano, coffee, cocoa powder, soy sauce, fish sauce, honey to the food processor.
  5. Add the cooled toasted chilies to the food processor.
  6. Blitz on high adding water or broth as needed.

Braising the meat

  1. Cube the meat into managable pieces
  2. Season the meat with salt and black pepper
  3. Add the prepared sauce into the meat and mix well
  4. Let marinate covered until ready to use, minimum 4 hours
  5. After marinating, put the meat with the sauce into a pressure cooker. Ensure that the meat is covered with the sauce
  6. Pressure cook on high for 45mins and release the pressure once cooking is finished
  7. Shred the cooked meat and reserve the stewing liquid

Taco

  1. Mix together chooped onion and cilantro
  2. Shred the cheese
  3. In a non stick pan on medium, dip the tortilla in the reserved stewing liquid and add it to the pan.
  4. Pan fry for 30secs and flip
  5. Add some of the meat and cheese on the tortilla. Fold the tortilla in half and cook until crispy and cheese is melted
  6. Serve with the onion and cilantro mixture. Along with salsa and guac.
